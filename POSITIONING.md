# POSITIONING.md - Angular Governance Program

This is the canonical positioning source for the repository.

## Core Statement

Governance-first stabilization program for Angular and Nx codebases that outgrew informal structure.

## Elevator Pitch

We reduce cost of change in Angular codebases by restoring architectural discipline — without rewriting your app.

## Target Scope

Niche Angular governance entry point, not full enterprise architecture stabilization.

## What We Do

- Establish and reinforce domain boundaries
- Improve ownership clarity and decision traceability
- Reduce cross-domain change surface
- Increase safety of upgrades through structural discipline
- Build a repeatable governance cadence with measurable progress

## What We Do Not Do

- Feature delivery ownership
- Rewrite programs
- Emergency throughput augmentation
- Backend/system-wide bottleneck ownership claims
- Dogmatic framework or architecture evangelism

## Primary Value

Reduce cost of change through structural governance.

## Key Outcomes and Metrics

### Outcomes
- Smaller pull requests with lower blast radius
- Safer upgrades and fewer regressions
- Clearer ownership per domain
- Reduced architectural drift

### Metrics
- Cross-domain file-touch rate per PR
- PR scope size trends
- Boundary violation trend (new vs resolved)
- Decision-log completion and reference rate
- Governance actions closed per cadence cycle

## Engagement Model

A weekly governance cadence:
- Baseline and risk mapping
- Governance setup and guardrails
- Weekly cadence for triage, decisions, and incremental actions
- Optional lower-intensity advisory once stability improves

## Audience Split

### For Managers
- Predictability, risk reduction, and measurable change economics

### For Technical Leads
- Practical governance mechanics: rules, logs, metrics, and safe sequencing

## AI Positioning

AI supports governance. It does not replace it.

Concrete usage examples:
- Drift detection support from PR and dependency signals
- Boundary-violation triage suggestions
- Ownership suggestion drafts from change patterns
- PR scope analysis to flag blast radius risk
- Decision-log summarization for governance reviews

## Messaging Guardrails

Prefer:
- governance-first stabilization
- cost-of-change reduction
- measurable structural progress
- safer upgrades and ownership clarity

Avoid:
- cleanup-shop storefront framing as primary message
- maintenance subscription framing
- sales-pitch language
- overclaims about whole-enterprise rescue
