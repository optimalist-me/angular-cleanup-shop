Is this just refactoring?

No. Refactoring is a tool. We reduce structural friction so change becomes cheaper, reviews faster, and delivery calmer.

Why only one day per week?

Consistency beats intensity. One fixed day keeps changes small, reviewable, and low-risk — without disrupting your delivery cadence.

Can you also build features?

No. We don’t take feature tickets. Your team ships features; we reduce the friction and risk around shipping them.

Do you work with Nx monorepos?

Yes. Nx makes boundaries enforceable. If you don’t use Nx, we can still enforce module boundaries via ESLint and CI checks.

Can’t we just use AI to clean this up?

AI helps, but it doesn’t replace governance. Cleanup still needs sequencing, boundary rules, and ownership decisions. Without that, AI can accelerate drift.

What is the day rate?

EUR 800 per day, fixed. You’re buying reduced entropy and lower cost-of-change — not hours logged.

What does success look like after 4-8 weeks?

Smaller PRs, clearer ownership, fewer cross-domain changes, and upgrades that feel safer because the structure is calmer.

When is this not a fit?

If you need urgent throughput, a rewrite, or temporary delivery capacity, this model will feel too deliberate.
