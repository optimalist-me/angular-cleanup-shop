Is this just refactoring?

No. We reduce structural friction so teams can move faster with less risk. Cleanup is the means; efficiency is the outcome.

Why only one day per week?

Consistency beats intensity. A weekly cadence keeps changes small, reviewable, and low-risk while protecting your delivery timeline.

Can you also build features?

No. This model is maintenance-first. Feature delivery remains with your team so structural improvements stay focused and predictable.

Do you work with Nx monorepos?

Yes. Nx is a first-class environment for this work: boundaries, ownership, and dependency rules become enforceable.

What is the day rate?

EUR 800 per day, fixed. The value is in reduced entropy and lower cost of change, not hours logged.

What does success look like after 4-8 weeks?

Smaller components, clearer ownership, safer PRs, and teams that ship with more confidence and less overhead.

When is this not a fit?

If you need urgent feature throughput, a rewrite, or temporary capacity, this model will feel too slow and deliberate.
