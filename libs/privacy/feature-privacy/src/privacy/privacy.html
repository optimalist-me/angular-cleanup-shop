Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

1. Data controller and contact

Angular Cleanup Shop is the data controller for personal data submitted through checkout and booking forms.

  • Data rights requests: support@angularcleanup.shop
  • Booking operations: bookings@angularcleanup.shop

2. Data we collect

We collect only the data needed to handle fit-check requests:

  • Name, work email, company
  • Team size, Angular version, Nx usage
  • Preferred dates and optional notes
  • Privacy policy acknowledgment (policy version and acceptance timestamp)

3. Why we process this data

  • To review and schedule your fit-check request
  • To communicate confirmations and operational updates by email
  • To maintain auditable records of privacy acknowledgment

4. Legal bases (GDPR)

  • Article 6(1)(b): processing necessary to take steps at your request before entering a service relationship
  • Article 6(1)(f): legitimate interests in operating, securing, and improving request handling and communications

5. Retention

Booking and checkout request records are kept for a maximum of twelve (12) months and are then deleted according to our retention policy.

6. Recipients and processors

Data is processed by Angular Cleanup Shop and by service providers acting as processors, including hosting infrastructure and SMTP email delivery providers.

7. International transfers

If data is transferred outside the EEA, we apply appropriate GDPR safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (or equivalent lawful mechanisms).

8. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, and data portability. You may also lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

To exercise rights, contact support@angularcleanup.shop.

9. Local storage and tracking

We use local storage only for cart state and theme preference. We do not use marketing or analytics cookies, third-party tracking pixels, or behavioral profiling tools. Service performance is measured using first-party operational booking metrics only.

10. Policy updates

We may update this policy to reflect legal, technical, or operational changes. The current version and date are always published on this page.
